Work

Monkey Jump was created by myself and a fellow classmate as part of week 9 of the Flatiron School Software Engineering program. It's a browser based video game running on vanilla Javascript with a Rails backend.

CaseWorks was created by myself and a fellow classmate as part of week 6 of the Flatiron School Software Engineering program. This application is a case management system for social workers serving the Austin, TX area and was built on the Ruby on Rails framework.

BestByMe was developed by myself and a fellow classmate as part of week 3 of the Flatiron School Software Engineering program. BestByMe is a CLI application designed for users who do not have the time or interest to spend hours reading reviews and researching businesses. When a user enters a business type and a zip code, the app responds with the top ten most highly rated business of that type in their area. This project was built using vanilla Ruby and the Yelp API.
